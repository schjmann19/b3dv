built with raylib

Fonts:
Custom fonts you can put in:
./assets/fonts/[fontname]/ttf/*.ttf

Commands:
/tp <x y z> - teleport player to coordinates
/setblock <x y z [block]> - set block at coordinates
/select <block> (stone|grass|dirt|wood|sand) - select block to place
/fly [enable|disable] - enable/disable flight
/noclip [enable|disable] - enable/disable noclip (going through blocks)
